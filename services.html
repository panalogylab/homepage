Social AI is a rapidly evolving field that explores the interaction between artificial intelligence (AI) and human social behaviour.
At Panalogy Lab, we believe that social AI has the potential to transform the way we live, work, and interact with the world around us. By developing cutting-edge insights and solutions that enable long-lived and evolving interactions between AI agents and people in complex social contexts, we are at the forefront of this exciting field.

Social AI Data Insights and Evidence-Based Advisory

Our panalogy approach to social AI research and design is grounded in a deep empirical understanding of behavioural complexity and uncertainties in real-world social systems. Panalogy Lab's empirical capability lies in their ability to process and analyse complex, multimodal data, extracting meaningful insights that can be applied to many different fields and domains. We can identify patterns, predict outcomes and risks, allow for tactical analysis, and identify talent, all of which can help individuals and organisations make more informed decisions to improve performance and increase efficiency. By combining multiple data sources, we can ensure proper observation of individual environmental and situational realities and use “reality mining” to improve human performance and quality of life. Our team has a deep understanding of the latest technological advances, including wearable nonintrusive instruments and surface electrodes, and the potential they hold for understanding human dynamics.,

Social AI Systems and Products

To build our social AI products, we use a plug-and-learn framework that combines augmented language models (ALMs) and reinforcement learning (RL) agents to create flexible and cost-effective solutions that can be tailored to different business situations. Our solutions can guide the new product design process from scratch, or they can be used to test the behaviours of a complete product before and after production launch.

Social AI Research and Development Frontier

Our research output seeks to push the frontier of social AI research, as we strive to create innovative solutions that break down disciplinary boundaries and explore the interconnectedness of all things. We recognise the importance of integrating different disciplinary areas and ideas to obtain a more comprehensive understanding of complex phenomena. Through our work, we hope to move one step closer to building artificial general intelligence (AGI) that benefits all of human society. As part of this effort, we are committed to exploring emerging technologies, particularly those entangled with or enabled by AI, including those further outside the mainstream like quantum computing, IoT, distributed technologies, expert systems, and symbolic logic, as part of our on-going research efforts.
