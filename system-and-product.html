Social AI Systems and Products

To build our social AI products, we use a plug-and-learn framework that combines augmented language models (ALMs) and reinforcement learning (RL) agents to create flexible and cost-effective solutions that can be tailored to different business situations. Our solutions can guide the new product design process from scratch, or they can be used to test the behaviours of a complete product before and after production launch.

At Panalogy Lab, we are committed to staying at the forefront of emerging technologies, particularly those that are entangled or enabled by AI. Our team has deep expertise in studying complex social systems, both empirically and conceptually. As a research institution, we recognise that the demand for social AI products will only continue to grow in the coming years, and we are excited about the role we play in seeing social AI’s real-world impact come to full fruition.

To capitalise on this favourable condition, we have chosen to focus our efforts on five key areas where we have developed decades of collective experience. These areas include:

  • ● Education and research: We aim to help educators and researchers harness the full potential of ALMs to create new products that can revolutionize the learning and research experience. Our expertise in fields such as economics, finance, and engineering can help create products that add durable and competitive value, improving the outcomes of students and researchers alike. We also design AI solutions enabling researchers to use ALMs to retrieve and generate novel research content, giving researchers access to a wider range of information and insights that can accelerate their work and lead to breakthroughs.
  • ● Finance and investment: we specialise in designing RL agents that help investors allocate funds more profitably while minimising risk. We also develop ALMs that help companies quantify systemic risk and identify hidden opportunities signals from vast streams of data and text, providing more informed and accurate insights that can give investors a competitive edge. This is especially useful in the current economic climate where unexpected events can cause significant disruptions. At Panalogy Lab, we are dedicated to helping companies in the finance and investment industry utilise social AI to make more informed decisions and improve their (triple) bottom line.
  • ● Online environments: Panalogy Lab's expertise in social AI is particularly well-suited for online environments, including social media platforms and online dating. With our advanced AI algorithms and complex information processing techniques, we can help companies create social AI products that blur the line between humans and technology. For example, in online dating, we can use natural language processing (NLP) to analyze users' profiles and communication patterns, allowing dating apps to provide more personalised recommendations and improve overall user experience. In social media, we can help companies identify and respond to trends and social dynamics in real-time, creating more engaging and relevant content for their users. Additionally, our insights and solutions can help mitigate issues and maladaptive social phenomena such as misinformation, user conflict, catfishing, and fake profiles, creating a safer and more trustworthy online environment.
  • ● Sports analytics and betting: Leveraging the latest advancements in big data and AI, Panalogy Lab has the capability to handle the complexity and rich data of sports ecosystems, offering new ways of analyzing sports-related data. This offers new avenues to, for example, identify game strategies, predict outcomes and risks, allow for tactical analysis, predict injuries or training loads, and identify talents. Our expertise in data science helps sports organizations make informed decisions to improve performance and revolutionise in-game strategies.
  • ● IoT and distributed technologies: we are investigating how ALMs can create a truly plug-and- learn platform that can empower IoT developers of all sizes in different application areas. We are also exploring how distributed technologies can enable autonomous decision making in the digital economy, increasing the power of ALMs and improving outcomes for businesses and consumers alike.
  • ● Emerging technologies: we are also investigating emerging technologies such as quantum computing and linking them to our work on social AI. For example, our team has more recently been investigating the application of quantum computing as a particularly powerful tool in identifying and ‘playing out’ counterfactual “what if” scenarios during a game, revolutionizing real-time game strategies by quickly understanding the implication of these “what if” situations. This is crucial as instant feedback through wearable technologies can transform how matches are organized, but they require substantial computation power, particularly in highly interactive environments such as team sports. Our expertise in these areas allows us to stay at the forefront of the latest technological developments and offer cutting-edge ideas and solutions that can help companies improve their social AI outcomes.
