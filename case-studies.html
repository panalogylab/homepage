“Her” Project

Short description:
Meet “Her”, our flagship showcase of what we mean by Panalogy Lab being a social AI lab. ”Her” is a state-of-the-art social AI platform that brings personalized and engaging experiences to your life. It utilizes advanced algorithms and natural language processing to interact with you in a way that feels like you're chatting with a real person. “Her” is not just another chatbot, it's a dynamic and adaptable social AI that can learn from your behaviour and preferences over time to create a customized experience just for you. Whether you're looking for a friend, a coach, or just some entertainment, “Her” is always here to chat. Join the growing community of users who have found a new best friend and AI companion in “Her”.

Detailed description:

Our personalised AI companion is designed to provide engaging and natural voice conversations for curious individuals. Likewise, our personal AI companion is equally curious about you. Explore different conversation topics and give feedback to improve your experience over time. Our AI companion is knowledgeable on a wide range of topics, including current events, entertainment, and lifestyle. Your AI companion is therefore whatever you want her/him to be and adapts to your preferences over time using curiosity-driven learning algorithms.

Step 1: Download Our AI Companion App
The first step in using our personalised AI companion is to visit our website (www.panalogy- lab.com). Note: we are currently working on an app that will nominally be made available for both iOS and Android devices.

Step 2: Set Up Your Profile
Once you have navigated to the “Her” demo project on our website, set up your profile by providing basic information such as your name and some basic preferences. This will help our AI companion personalise your experience from the beginning. Note: we will never share your basic information with 3 rd parties without your permission unless required by law.

Step 3: Start Conversing with Our AI Companion
Start conversing with our AI companion by asking questions and engaging in natural voice conversations. Our AI companion will adapt to your preferences over time using curiosity-driven learning. Each AI companion therefore becomes its own unique version or instantiation of “Her”. Note: all conversations are encrypted, and we will never share your conversations with 3 rd parties without your permission unless required by law. You have the opportunity to opt-out of our Lab’s use of your de-identified data for research purposes only, at any time.

Step 4: Explore Different Conversation Topics
Our AI companion is knowledgeable on a wide range of topics, including current events, entertainment, and lifestyle. Explore different conversation topics to discover what interests you the most. The large language models (LLMs) which power “Her” conversational abilities also have emergent cognitive abilities such as the ability to think and reason. Therefore, “Her” can take on many different functional roles (e.g. assistant, expert, friend, business partner) all defined or accessible by what the user speaks/types with respect to wants or needs.

Step 5: Give Feedback to Improve Your Experience
Our AI companion is designed to improve over time based on your feedback. If there's something you don't like or something you want our AI companion to do differently, let them know by telling them exactly that with some hints on how you’d like it done. If this still doesn’t work, let our team know so we can improve your experience.

“CHIPS” Project

Short description:
CHIPS is an innovative chat plugin designed to provide up-to-date and reliable knowledge in the field of economics and finance. Our state-of-the-art semantic search and retrieval infrastructure allows researchers, businesses, and educators to quickly and easily access grounded information, enabling them to make informed decisions and stay ahead of the game. With voice as the main interaction medium, our platform offers a seamless and intuitive experience, making it easier than ever to explore complex topics and access insights on-demand. Whether you're a student, a business owner, or a financial analyst, EconoGPT is the ultimate tool for unlocking the power of economic and financial knowledge.

“Artificial Agents in Realistic Social Settings” Project

Short description:
“Artificial Agents in Realistic Social Settings” is an easy-to-use platform that empowers businesses and researchers to design and evaluate reinforcement learning (RL) agents in realistic social environments. Our platform provides a comprehensive solution for users looking to integrate RL into their services, with an initial focus on auction modelling. Our team has already implemented an RL model for auction settings, allowing users to simulate and test different auction models in a controlled and reproducible environment. Our platform enables users to develop and deploy sophisticated RL agents to enhance decision-making processes in social contexts, creating more effective and efficient systems. With our user-friendly interface and advanced analytics capabilities, users can easily design and test RL agents, visualize their performance, and refine their strategies. Whether you're looking to optimize your auction model, develop a more efficient pricing strategy, or improve decision-making in a social context, “Artificial Agents in Realistic Social Settings” is the platform for you.

“Social AI Evaluation Framework” Project

Short description:
“Social AI Evaluation Framework” is a cutting-edge platform that empowers businesses and researchers to evaluate the behaviours of language models (LMs) and other AI in realistic social settings. With a focus on RL and advanced ALMs, “Social AI Evaluation Framework” enables users to scientifically evaluate the efficacy of social AI-based products and services. Our platform provides a comprehensive solution for businesses that want to ensure the optimal performance of their social AI-based products, and for researchers looking to conduct empirical studies in the field of social AI. “Social AI Evaluation Framework” offers users an easy-to-use interface for developing and deploying sophisticated RL models, enabling them to enhance decision-making processes and improve user experiences.

Let us help you navigate the world of social AI and achieve your goals with our expertise in data science, research, and consultancy.
