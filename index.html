We Are Panalogy Lab

At Panalogy, we view social AI as a rapidly evolving field that develops long-lived AI systems to operate in complex social environments. Our long-term focus is on designing interdisciplinary frameworks to study emergent behaviors of these systems while they interact with humans and other artifacts in their social environments. The behavioral insights gained from this process in turn help improve the systems’ development in directions that better align with ever-changing human intentions and values. We believe that this behavioral approach will make significant contributions to alignment research and will become increasingly relevant when the social aspect of AI becomes more complex.

Our Research

At Panalogy Lab, we believe that social AI has the potential to transform the way we live, work, and interact with the world around us. By developing cutting-edge insights and solutions that enable evolving yet aligned interactions between AI agents and people in complex social contexts, we are at the forefront of this exciting field.
