From Theory to Practice

Bridging theory and practice, we drive innovation from concepts into real-world solutions.

Our Mission

Building better AI by better understanding people.

At Panalogy Lab, our mission is to unlock the transformative power of Social AI. We design AI systems grounded in real-world human behavior—flexible, adaptive, and aligned to human needs.

We’re guided by three beliefs:

  • Science-first: Every product we build is underpinned by empirical research.
  • Human-centered: We prioritize the complexity and dignity of human life in every system we design.
  • Exploration-driven: Our best tools have come from curiosity and experimentation.

👉 Learn more: What is Social AI?




Our Journey

From curiosity to capability.

  • Late 2022: Formed our interdisciplinary founding team.
  • Early 2023: Released the first prototype of LifeOS.
  • Mid 2023: Released the first prototype of SurveyLM.
  • 2024: First research publication/s using our tools.
  • Late 2024: Joined the Inductive accelerator program.
  • 2024–25: Launching pilots and expanding Social AI offerings into education, R&D, and startup ecosystems.



Meet The Core Team

Panalogy Lab is led by five Co-Founders who bring together academic excellence, start-up drive, technical capability, and cross-sector experience. United by a shared vision, we build socially impactful AI solutions with agility and purpose.

Coined by Benno as "a home for the mind," Panalogy Lab is our creative outlet for applying behavioural insight and AI innovation to real-world challenges.





Professor Benno Torgler

Co-Founder/Director
Google Scholar Profile

Benno is a leading behavioural economist and social science researcher, known for founding QUT's Centre for Behavioural Economics and co-leading the ARC BITA Centre. With over 300 academic contributions, he brings deep interdisciplinary insight and policy engagement experience. As Panalogy Lab's Director, Benno helps shape the research vision and AI strategy that underpin our social impact work.





Dr. Steve Bickley

Co-Founder/Operations

What Does “Panalogy” Mean?

A synthetic term, for a powerful way of thinking.

Panalogy (n.) — from pan (“all”) and analogy (“relational structure”) — is a method of insight generation through deep, multi-contextual comparison.

It’s how we work: by bridging fields, modeling alignment, and building tools that integrate systems and people.
